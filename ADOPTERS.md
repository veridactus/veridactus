## Conformance

Organizations can certify VERIDACTUS compatibility by passing the [conformance test suite](veridactus/conformance/). Certified implementations may use the "VERIDACTUS-Compatible" badge.

---

## Join the Adopter List

1. Deploy VERIDACTUS in your environment
2. Pass the [conformance tests](https://github.com/veridactus/veridactus/tree/main/veridactus/conformance)
3. Submit a PR adding your organization to the table above
4. Receive the VERIDACTUS adopter badge for your website
